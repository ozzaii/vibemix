[#announcements]

vibemix is live — a free, open-source AI co-host that listens to your live DJ sets.

real DJ friend in your ear, not voice-assistant slop. built by DJs, runs locally on Mac + Windows, and your audio doesn't leave your machine without you knowing.

→ listens to master output (BlackHole on mac, WASAPI loopback on win)
→ watches djay Pro / Rekordbox
→ reads MIDI from ~10 mapped controllers (DDJ-FLX4 out of box)
→ reacts in real-time as hype-man (party) or coach (feedback)

Show us your sets in #showcase. Bugs in #bugs. Pulls welcome.
