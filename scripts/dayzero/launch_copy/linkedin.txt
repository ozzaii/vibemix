Open-sourcing vibemix today — Bravoh's first OSS release.

vibemix is a free AI co-host for live DJ sets. It listens to your master output, watches your DJ software's screen, and reacts as either hype-man or coach — never hallucinating, never breaking flow.

Built on Gemini 3 Flash + Gemini Embedding 2 with real-time grounding from audio, screen, MIDI, and now-playing metadata. The full pipeline lives in your local machine; only Gemini API calls hit the wire.

Why open-source? Two reasons:
1. Trust. DJs run their gear close to the chest. Showing the code is the only way to credibly say "your sets stay local."
2. Warmup. Bravoh's bigger product (AI Artist OS) launches in March. vibemix proves the grounded-AI thesis with a narrow, polished utility first.
