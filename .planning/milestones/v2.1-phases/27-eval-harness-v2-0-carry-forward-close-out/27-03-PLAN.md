archive.org search filter (per RESEARCH §Open Questions #2 + WebSearch verified):
- Advanced search: `licenseurl:(*publicdomain* OR *cc0* OR *publicdomain/zero*) AND mediatype:(audio) AND subject:("dj mix" OR "dj set" OR techno OR house OR "hard tek")`
- Manual filter: ≥ 25 min duration, mixed-set format (not single-track)
- API endpoint: https://archive.org/advancedsearch.php?q=<query>&fl[]=identifier,title,licenseurl,duration&output=json&rows=20

CCMixter:
- API: http://ccmixter.org/api/query?f=json&search_type=tags&t=<tag>&lic=open
