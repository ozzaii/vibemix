## Overview

v2.0 = Bravoh's first OSS release ammunition. The "Research-Driven Ship" milestone took the 12-bucket research swarm output (`.planning/research/v2-buckets/SYNTHESIS.md`) and absorbed outstanding v0.1.0 ship work (recording browser, sign+notarize, README, day-zero ops) into one bulky milestone, plus six anti-slop research-driven feature blocks: Hard Tek detectors, Evidence Registry + citation grammar, Latency Stack v1, Citation Linter ENFORCEMENT, mascot anticipation layer, djay Pro overlay, 10-SKU MIDI library, Pyrekordbox XML import, viral demo + day-zero ops.

The milestone landed the **central anti-slop product thesis** in code: every spoken Gemini reaction is now citation-validated against an in-memory EvidenceRegistry written synchronously by the state refresh loop and EventDetector — un-cited responses strip and fall back to a 40-OPUS ack bank, keeping the AI honest by construction.

10 of 12 phases shipped end-to-end Claude-side. Phase 15 Plan 04 (Kaan UAT ear-test) and full Phase 16 (Kaan's DJ ear hallucination gate) deferred to Kaan-action per autonomous-fully mode and `project_phase_16_kaan_dj_testing` memory. No critical code-side blockers stopping v2.0 RC cut.
