## Why deferred

The current Phase 25 pyrekordbox pin is shallow XML parsing only (`--no-deps` recipe + dormant SQLCipher per CONTEXT D-01). Going deep means re-activating the SQLCipher path for db6 reads (Rekordbox 6 binary library DB), plus richer ANLZ binary parsing for waveform color cues, hot-cue timing, and beatgrid pulls. Install-impact is Red (SQLCipher prebuilt wheels are platform-fragmented). v3.1 distribution polish does not justify the surface expansion.
