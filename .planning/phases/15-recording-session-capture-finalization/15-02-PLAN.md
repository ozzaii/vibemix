| T-15-02-02 | Information Disclosure | Webview reads file outside recordings/ via crafted asset:// URL | mitigate | Tauri assetProtocol scope `$APPDATA/vibemix/recordings/**` enforces; Tauri resolves real paths (no symlink escape). |
