80% recessive · the deep quiet
  • Cinematic center-vignette — eye stays in the middle, edges fall to absolute black. Background pulls the room inward.
  • Multi-layer film grain — coarser dust (0.42 freq) + fine grain (0.92 freq) blended at overlay. Texture has depth, not flatness.

  • Reduced glow — every shadow/halo distance cut ~40%. Restraint signals quality.
  • Cool void stack — five tints from #000 to #11141c. Slight blue undertone for the "icy" read.
  • Restrained rave washes — 5 colored gradients at 2-5% alpha. Bleed through glass via backdrop-saturate. Barely there.
