01 the promise

a real DJ struggles with a transition. an AI voice cuts in, calm and useful. the DJ trusts it. the drop lands. that's the whole movie.

02 aesthetic intent
  • shoot in dark — clubs, studios, blacked-out rooms. backlit, never flat-lit.
  • vibemix UI appears as a real chip floating in frame — not a cutaway, a layer.
  • mascot Avery cameos in 3 frames. it watches. it reacts. it never speaks on camera.
  • no AI-slop. no purple gradients. no "futuristic synthwave grid." just amber on charcoal, and one DJ.
03 sound brief

original techno bed (commissioned, not licensed — Kaan provides). 140 bpm. F minor. builds at 0:14, drops at 0:18. Avery TTS over the music — clean, off-axis, like a friend leaning into your ear.
