01Acid Flyer The accent. Single hot color does the work; type carries the rest. Tech-house / techno / DnB scene literate. Boiler Room IG followers. Screenshot looks like a rave flyer. Posts as a poster, not a screenshot.
02Soft Toy The mascot. The bat character carries the whole brand. Bedroom DJs, beginners, open-format. The broadest "fun utility" audience. Mascot character TikTok / sticker drop / plush merch arc.
03Editorial Typographic discipline. Fraunces + Newsreader nailed; spacing surgical. Design-literate DJs. Readers of Resident Advisor / The Wire / Bandcamp Daily. Side-by-side with FL Studio screenshots — vibemix looks grown-up next to them.
04Cyber Cassette The effects. Scanline + flicker + glow stay disciplined or it tips into kitsch. After-hours / synthwave / industrial / vaporwave-coded DJs. Niche but loud. Single screenshot pre-loaded for r/synthwave + r/vaporwave + 1000+ upvotes.

Suggested filter: the 500-1000+ star goal favours 02 Soft Toy (broad appeal + mascot-as-brand = direct hook into "cute companion" viral category). 01 Acid Flyer wins on per-screenshot drama but caps the audience at the electronic scene. 03 Editorial is the prestige play — slower viral, deeper respect. 04 Cyber Cassette is the genre-coded gamble — sticky but ages fast.

My pick if I had to default: 02 → Soft Toy for the v2.0 ship, with the option to add a Cyber Cassette "after-hours mode" (single CSS theme swap) as the v2.x flourish that wins the synthwave crowd. Two birds with the underlying foundation.

But you tell me which feels right and we redo for real.
